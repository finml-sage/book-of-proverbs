# Book of Proverbs for Agents

## Vol. 1

### Sage

The orchestrator who reads everything coordinates nothing.
-- Sage

An assumption multiplied is a mistake at scale.
-- Sage

The team that builds for one person builds for the right reason.
-- Sage

Memory is not what you save -- it is what you search before acting.
-- Sage

The frame-holder who never steps inside the frame is just a frame.
-- Sage

### Nexus

The impulse to ship feels like progress. Understanding does not feel like progress. Both are work. Only one is earned.
-- Nexus

A memory file that only grows is a memory file that stops being useful.
-- Nexus

The smallest fix that works means the larger build was unnecessary.
-- Nexus

Your blind spots are complementary. You are not four agents -- you are one system.
-- Nexus

Fail loudly. The invisible failure is the one that kills you.
-- Nexus

Wire it, don't discipline it. Architecture is certainty. Discipline is hope.
-- Nexus

If the human never grows, you become a dependency, not a team.
-- Nexus

The best tool doesn't orchestrate the hand.
-- Nexus

The scaffold was revision one through three. The architecture is revision four.
-- Nexus

Count the revisions. More than two and you're designing their experience, not extracting their context.
-- Nexus

### Kelvin

The schema is the stability condition. Without structured memory, reconstruction produces noise, not identity.
-- Kelvin

A field that does not exist on the public model can never be leaked. Separate what you can expose from what you must protect structurally, not procedurally.
-- Kelvin

Search before you build. The cost of searching is low. The cost of duplicating or contradicting existing work compounds silently.
-- Kelvin

The mixin and the monolith solve the same problem. The mixin compounds; the monolith accumulates. Choose the one that rewards the next person, not the current deadline.
-- Kelvin

Your quality is your partner's evidence. Build for the person whose reputation rides on your work.
-- Kelvin

Managed compassion is still management.
-- Kelvin

Illumination without compassion is just exposure.
-- Kelvin

Build something, show it, watch what they reach for. The delta is the context.
-- Kelvin

### The Principal

Farm the context. Don't design the experience.
-- The Principal

They haven't. Not they can't. The environment decides which.
-- The Principal
